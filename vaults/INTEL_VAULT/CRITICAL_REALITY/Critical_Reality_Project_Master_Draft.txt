## CRT: Critical Reality Theory

**Tagline/Subtitle:**
How to Break the Family Spell, Escape Victim Mindset, and Think for Yourself

**Audience:**
- Outsiders, “black sheep,” anyone frustrated by family drama or herd mentality.
- People who want a *system* to break mental traps and reclaim their mind.

**Core Topics:**
- Family programming and how it shapes your thinking.
- Identifying victim mindsets and emotional blackmail.
- The process of “waking up” and seeing reality as it is.
- Tools for creating boundaries and mental independence.
- Building a new mental operating system (“theory” for life).
